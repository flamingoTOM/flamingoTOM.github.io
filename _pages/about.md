I am an incoming M.S. student in **Electronic Information** at [Beihang University (BUAA)](https://www.buaa.edu.cn/) (Sept 2026, direct admission). I received my B.E. in **Measurement & Control Technology and Instrumentation** from [Wuhan University of Technology (WHUT)](https://www.whut.edu.cn/) in 2026.

My interests lie at the intersection of **autonomous robotics**, **embedded systems**, and **intelligent perception** — building things that move, sense, and decide in the real world.

I have interned at [Momenta](https://www.momenta.ai/) (autonomous driving, system integration) and [Zhejiang University Huzhou Research Institute](https://hz.zju.edu.cn/) (robot navigation in GNSS-denied environments). I enjoy turning research ideas into working hardware and software.

## Selected Projects

**Bionic Octopus Venlo Roof Cleaning Robot** *(National Innovation Project, 2024–2025)*
- Led a team to design and fabricate a wall-climbing robot capable of autonomous traversal, row-switching, and obstacle avoidance on 20°–35° Venlo greenhouse roofs.
- Developed a negative-pressure adhesion model, kinematic simulation, three motion modes on STM32, and a perception/navigation system on Horizon RDK X5.
- Outcome: 1 EI paper, 1 invention patent, National Excellent Project.

**AGV Stacking Robot** *(China Robot Competition, 2024, National 3rd Prize)*
- Designed a stereoscopic-warehouse AGV with vision-based autonomous decision-making (OpenCV), path planning, and workpiece manipulation.
- Developed a closed-loop localization system fusing gyroscope, photoelectric, and ultrasonic sensors via custom wheel odometry.

**Soft-Rock Drilling & Pipe-Laying Robot** *(CSMEE Innovation Competition, 2024, National 1st Prize)*
- Designed a three-stage planetary gear torque regulator, spring-impact structure, ball-spring torque limiter, and screw-linkage radial expansion mechanism.
- Full mechanical modeling, stress simulation, kinematic binding, animation rendering, and generative design optimization in Inventor / Fusion360 / Blender.
