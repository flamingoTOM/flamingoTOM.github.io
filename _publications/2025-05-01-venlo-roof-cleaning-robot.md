This paper presents a bionic octopus-inspired wall-climbing robot designed for autonomous cleaning of Venlo-type greenhouse roofs.

**Key contributions:**
- Novel motion method enabling continuous row-switching and obstacle avoidance on 20°–35° Venlo-profile surfaces.
- Negative-pressure adhesion model and kinematic simulation validated on a full-scale prototype.
- Three motion modalities implemented on STM32; perception and autonomous navigation system deployed on Horizon RDK X5 fusing multi-sensor data.
